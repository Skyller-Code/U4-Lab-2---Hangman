Rules of Hangman

  1. There is a hidden phrase that the player must guess that is marked by underscores in the middle of the screen.
  2. You have 7 chances to guess the phrase fully before the man is hanged.
  3. Each incorrect guess will result in a part of the hangman being drawn.
  4. If you guess the phrase before the hangman is fully drawn, you win and you and the hangman get to live happily ever after!
  5. If all of the hangman's limbs are drawn, then you'll have to live with the guilt of killing a man...
